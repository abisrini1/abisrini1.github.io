About

I’m a graduate student at Johns Hopkins University pursuing an M.S.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering, focused on computer vision, medical imaging, and machine learning. I work on research projects in CT denoising, medical image segmentation, and generative models for healthcare applications.

Projects & Research

Low-Dose CT Denoising with Plug-and-Play + VAE Prior

Built a lightweight VAE and integrated it into a Plug-and-Play optimization framework for low-dose CT denoising. Evaluated against TV baselines with simulated Poisson–Gaussian noise.

3D Circle of Willis Segmentation from MRA

Working on 3D vascular segmentation of the Circle of Willis from MRA volumes using deep learning and representation learning techniques.

Chest X-ray Augmentation using GANs and VAEs

Compared CVAE and cGAN-based augmentation for imbalanced chest X-ray classification and evaluated synthetic image quality and downstream performance.
